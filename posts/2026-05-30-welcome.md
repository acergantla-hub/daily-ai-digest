# Welcome to Daily AI Digest

This site is your **daily briefing** on everything happening in the AI agent ecosystem. We cut through the noise and bring you the most important, actionable updates — every single day.

## What You'll Find Here

Each daily post covers:

- **Source** — Where the news came from (X, YouTube, Hacker News, blogs)
- **Why It Matters** — Impact analysis in 2-3 sentences
- **Recommended Response** — What you should do about it
- **Draft Hook** — A ready-to-use social media post if you want to share it

## Topics We Track

- AI Agents & Agent Frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, OpenClaw, Hermes)
- Claude Code & AI Coding Assistants
- Open-source AI Tools & Models
- AI Automation & Workflows
- Startup & Industry News

## How This Works

Posts are written in Markdown, generated with a lightweight Python build script, and hosted on **Cloudflare Pages for free**. No database, no server bills, no complexity.

New posts appear at the top. Check back daily.
