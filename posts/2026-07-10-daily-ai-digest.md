### Why This Matters Today

Today's digest reveals the AI agent ecosystem reaching a maturation point. The dominant theme isn't just new agent capabilities — it's **infrastructure for production deployment**. Salesforce's Slackbot and Anthropic's Cowork target business users directly, while Lyzr's $100M raise-by-agent demonstrates commercial viability. The shift from "agents for coders" to "agents for everyone" is accelerating.

This infrastructure — proper evaluation, secure deployment, and user-friendly interfaces — is what separates 2026 from the early agent hype cycles.
