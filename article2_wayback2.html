U.S. government will decide who gets to use latest upgrade to ChatGPT

The Trump administration came to power preaching a laissez-faire approach to AI but has lately increased oversight of the industry.

June 26, 2026 at 5:52 p.m. EDTYesterday at 5:52 p.m. EDT
6 min
Make us preferred on Google
By Gerrit De Vynck
and 
Isaac Arnsdorf

SAN FRANCISCO — The federal government will vet companies that want to access the latest technology developed by ChatGPT-maker OpenAI, in a major expansion of the Trump administration’s regulation of Silicon Valley.
